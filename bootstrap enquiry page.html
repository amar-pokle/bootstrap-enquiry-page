About Us

Vision & Mission

Vision:

To become the leader in enhancing the employability skills and providing the industry with targeted and productive manpower.

Mission:

Providing content rich learning programs and interactions to Individuals, that are Innovative, Insightful, Transforming, Empowering and helping them to identify their uniqueness and strengths and putting them to best possible use, with a definite sense of purpose.

COMPANY PROFILE

Incorporated in 2011, Prognoz is a private limited company and focuses on providing employability skills and enhancing the soft skills and the technical skills of school dropouts to technical graduates and working professionals, through our meticulously design courses, established skill development processes and competent faculties.

The company is ISO 9001:2015 certified and led by highly qualified and experienced team who is passionate about teaching and mentoring. Our skill development process constitutes of uniquely blended training programs of technical and soft skills which transforms a trainee to become employable and to take real life challenges in industries.
